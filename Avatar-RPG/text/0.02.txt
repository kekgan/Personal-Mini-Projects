You are Kuya, a young earthbender born in the town of Gao Ling in the Earth Kingdom with a strong sense of justice and an even stronger skill in earthbending.
At a mere 16 years of age, you have bested every earthbending master in Gaoling and its bordering towns.
Although your family wasn't particularly well off, your earthbending skills put you on the map.
This is the story of how you found out you were more than just an earthbending master.
