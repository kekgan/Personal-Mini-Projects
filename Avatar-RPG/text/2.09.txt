Out of the corner of your eye, you notice someone dressed in a full blue cloak.
He sprints behind a building and out of your line of sight.
Curious, you take your leave from the stage and take after him.
He leads you into the forest and you quickly realise something is wrong.
"Show yourself, coward!", you yell into the forest.
The hooded figure steps out from behind a large tree.
He pulls back his hood and you realise he isn't any hooded figure.
He's a spirit; hence the blue glow.
And he's an Air Nomad, hence the newly-discovered blue tatoo on his head.
