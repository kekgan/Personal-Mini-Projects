Fire.
Air.
Water.
Earth.
No matter if you lived in the yesteryears of Avatar Aang, the age of Avatar Korra, or the days of legends yet to come.
It has been the Avatar's responsibility to achieve balance and equality in the world since time immemorial.
However, perhaps it is the very presence and strength of the Avatar, that invites challenge and disorder.
Avatar Korra achieved much in her tenure to prove such naysayers wrong, even going as far as to unify the physical and spirit worlds permanently.
Nonetheless, her achievements did not come without their own sets of repercussions.
With the new Avatar's tether to her past lives being all but severed, will she be able to overcome her foes and fulfill her duty?
